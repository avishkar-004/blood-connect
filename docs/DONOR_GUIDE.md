## üìñ Detailed Feature Guide

### 1. Donation Camps

**Purpose**: Find and book donation camps in your area.

#### How to Use:

1. **Navigate**: Click "Donation Camps" in the sidebar/menu

2. **Browse Camps**: View all upcoming camps with details:
   - Camp name (e.g., "City Central Blood Drive")
   - Date and time
   - Location with full address
   - Organizer name
   - Available slots

3. **Check Availability**:
   - Green badge: Many slots available
   - Yellow badge: Few slots left
   - Red badge: Nearly full
   - "Fully Booked": No slots available

4. **Book a Camp**:
   - Click "Register Now" button
   - Confirmation message appears
   - Slot count decreases
   - You'll receive a confirmation notification

5. **What Happens After Booking**:
   - Booking saved in your account
   - Slot reserved for you
   - Reminder notification before camp date
   - Appears in your dashboard

#### Camp Information Details:

**Date & Time**
- Full date displayed (e.g., "Monday, December 25, 2024")
- Time slot (e.g., "9:00 AM - 3:00 PM")

**Location**
- Full address provided
- Usually at hospitals, schools, or community centers

**Organizer**
- Who is hosting the camp
- Contact information available

**Slots**
- Shows available/total (e.g., "15/50")
- Book early to secure your spot

#### Important Notes:

- ‚ö†Ô∏è You can only book if you're eligible to donate
- ‚ö†Ô∏è One booking per camp per person
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Arrive 15 minutes before your time slot

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page. Enter your email to receive reset instructions.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Email is your username and can't be changed. Contact support if needed.

**Q: Can I change my blood type in my profile?**
A: Blood type can't be self-modified for safety. Contact admin with proof if it's incorrect.

### Donations & Eligibility

**Q: How long must I wait between donations?**
A: 90 days (approximately 3 months) between blood donations for safety.

**Q: I'm eligible but the camp won't let me book. Why?**
A: The camp may be fully booked, or there may be technical issues. Try another camp or contact support.

**Q: Does donating platelets have the same waiting period?**
A: This system currently tracks whole blood donations only. Platelet donation rules differ.

**Q: Can I donate if I have a cold?**
A: You should be healthy and symptom-free. Consult camp medical staff before donating.

### Donation Camps

**Q: What should I bring to a donation camp?**
A:
- Valid photo ID
- This app confirmation (if available)
- Donor card (if you have one)
- List of medications (if any)

**Q: How long does donation take?**
A: The actual donation takes 8-10 minutes, but plan for 45-60 minutes total (registration, screening, recovery).

**Q: Can I cancel a camp booking?**
A: Currently, cancellation isn't available through the app. Contact the organizer directly if you can't attend.

**Q: What if I miss my booked camp?**
A: Your slot will be released. You can book another camp anytime.

### Notifications

**Q: Why am I getting so many notifications?**
A: Adjust your notification preferences in Settings. You can turn off specific alert types.

**Q: I didn't receive a notification for a blood request. Why?**
A: Check your notification settings are enabled. Also verify your blood type is correctly set.

**Q: How quickly should I respond to urgent blood requests?**
A: Urgent requests should be responded to within a few hours if possible. Emergency requests need immediate attention.

### Privacy & Safety

**Q: Who can see my personal information?**
A: Only your name, blood type, location, and availability are visible to recipients. Phone/email are private unless you choose to share.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, all data is stored securely. We never share your information with third parties without consent.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, go to Settings > Danger Zone > Delete Account. This is permanent and cannot be undone.

## üí° Tips for Donors

### Before Donating

1. **Eat Well**: Have a full meal 2-3 hours before
2. **Hydrate**: Drink plenty of water
3. **Rest**: Get good sleep the night before
4. **Avoid Alcohol**: No alcohol 24 hours before donation
5. **Check Eligibility**: Use app to confirm you're eligible

### During Donation

1. **Relax**: Stay calm, the process is quick
2. **Breathe Deeply**: Helps with relaxation
3. **Alert Staff**: Mention any discomfort immediately
4. **Don't Look**: If needles make you uncomfortable

### After Donation

1. **Rest**: Sit for 10-15 minutes before leaving
2. **Eat & Drink**: Have snacks and fluids provided
3. **Avoid Exercise**: No heavy lifting for 24 hours
4. **Keep Bandage On**: For at least 4 hours
5. **Update App**: Check if your next eligible date updated

### Maximizing Your Impact

1. **Regular Donations**: Set reminders for every 90 days
2. **Bring Friends**: Encourage others to donate
3. **Stay Healthy**: Maintain good health to remain eligible
4. **Spread Awareness**: Share your experience on social media
5. **Emergency Response**: Enable urgent notifications for emergency cases

### Health Requirements

You can donate if you:
- ‚úÖ Are 18-65 years old
- ‚úÖ Weigh at least 50 kg
- ‚úÖ Are in good health
- ‚úÖ Haven't donated in last 90 days
- ‚úÖ Don't have recent tattoos/piercings (6 months)

You cannot donate if you:
- ‚ùå Have cold, flu, or infection
- ‚ùå Are pregnant or breastfeeding
- ‚ùå Have certain medical conditions
- ‚ùå Took specific medications recently
- ‚ùå Have recently traveled to malaria-risk areas
